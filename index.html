About Us

Who We Are

Nursing is science and art, one that is dedicated to the health of the people. It is an applied science based on biological, physical, and behavioral disciplines. By acquiring the knowledge of science and art of nursing and learning to put this knowledge into practice, the nurse executes the goal of providing comfort with solicitude, compassion, promoting optimal level of health and acting effectively during period of illness.

Vision

The Allied institute of nursing will offer a wide array of educational opportunity including undergraduate to post graduate studies, for the student’s population that will embrace with exhilaration and promote empowerment of nursing professional which will exert positive impact in the dynamic health care delivery system locally, nationally, and globally.

Mission

To offer an unparalleled learning environment in healthcare industry with a determination to produce ethical and dedicated qualified professionals by providing quality Nursing education.
